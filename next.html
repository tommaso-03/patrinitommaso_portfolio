NEXT / Rebranding / 2024

NEXT Cooling SA is the result of a complete visual identity renewal following the merger of Nooter Eriksen’s NEXT with IWC Cooling. This rebranding project focused on modernizing the visual identity and communication strategy to position the newly formed entity as an innovative leader in the industrial cooling sector.

The work encompassed a comprehensive brand redesign, including a logo relook, defining the brand identity and establishing guidelines, and creating mockups for key physical applications. The primary objective was to create a flexible, distinctive system capable of supporting the merged company's growth and reflecting its future-oriented vision.

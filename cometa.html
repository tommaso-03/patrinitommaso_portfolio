Cometa / Reportage / 2025

Cometa, founded in 1986 in Como, is a visionary educational community where work becomes a pathway to dignity, empowerment, and personal transformation. This documentary reportage explores how Cometa uplifts young people facing challenges, including those with disabilities, by blending tradition, innovation, and inclusion.

The story highlights Contrada degli Artigiani, where crafts like woodworking and restoration inspire growth and purpose; Il Pane di Sandro, a bakery that merges professional training with social inclusion; and Anagramma, a café in the gardens of Villa Bernasconi, where hospitality fosters education and belonging.

MTP600 / Editorial Design / 2025

MTP600 is a typographic editorial project developed as a brand extension proposal for CEME, a company specializing in technical pumps and valves. The publication redefines the visual identity of industrial components by presenting the MTP600 pump as a graphic subject with aesthetic and design value.

Featuring a brutalist, black-and-white layout, the project transforms technical specifications into a contemporary visual language through bold typography, threshold effects, and dynamic compositions. Designed as part of a collectible monthly series, it offers a new narrative tool for the company—parallel to corporate communication and focused on revealing the unseen design behind industrial production.​​​

Urban Visions / Editorial Book / 2023

For this project, my team and I delved into the legacy of public utility graphics, focusing on Albe Steiner's social design and its impact on ISIA Urbino. We explored how Steiner’s ideas, carried forward by students like Massimo Dolcini, shaped modern public design. This research culminated in a meta three-dimensional exhibition titled "Urban Visions," connecting Steiner’s and Dolcini’s work with contemporary digital urban spaces and interactive design.

My contributions included writing the research text and designing the book layout, drawing inspiration from Steiner's functional Swiss style and the public signage central to our study.

Pagani Huayra / Photographic Workshop / 2025

I had the opportunity to take part in the “Huayra Roadster BC: Portrait of a Hypercar” workshop, held at Politecnico di Milano in collaboration with Pagani Automobili.

Led by Luigi Ganzerli and photographer Paolo Carlini, the session focused on automotive photography and offered the chance to work directly with the Pagani Huayra Roadster BC. These are some of the photos I took during the workshop.

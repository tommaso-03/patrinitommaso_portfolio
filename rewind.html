Rewind / Music Videoclip / 2025

"Rewind" is a song by Camilla Buzzetti, an emerging artist and student at CPM, for which I directed the official music video. With a country girl style inspired by great artists like Taylor Swift, the song is an anthem of personal freedom and inner rebirth after tough times. The visuals reflect this energy, capturing the strength and determination to start over. Produced by Alta Vibe & Alari Park Studios.

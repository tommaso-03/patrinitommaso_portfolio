Milan Design Week / Reportage / 2025

Milan Design Week is one of the most important global events for design and innovation. Each year, alongside the main Salone del Mobile exhibition, the Fuorisalone takes over various districts of Milan, transforming the city into a vibrant stage for exhibitions, art installations, and presentations of new projects and trends. It’s a unique opportunity to discover the latest in design, architecture, and art.

Here is a small collection of my photographic reportage from the 2025 Milan Design Week, offering a glimpse into the dynamic and creative energy of the Fuorisalone.

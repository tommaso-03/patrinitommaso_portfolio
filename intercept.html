Intercept - Sexting Coercion / Guerrilla Research / 2024

This project was part of the WeSayStop communication campaign by Politecnico di Milano, focused on raising awareness about gender-based violence and discrimination. Our team addressed the issue of Sexting Coercion. As part of the research phase, we produced a video intercept to explore perceptions of the topic within the university, interviewing around 80 students to assess their awareness and sensitivity.


As video-maker, I directed the filming and captured portraits of all participants to enhance our research and documentation. The guerrilla-style interviews featured direct questions on sexting coercion, with an interactive element allowing students to express their emotions freely. This project deepened our understanding of the issue and helped create a compelling visual narrative for awareness.

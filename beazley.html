Beazley / Corporate Event / 2025

Beazley is a global insurance company known for hosting curated events in iconic locations around the world. For their annual Milan stop, they chose the rooftop cocktail terrace of Maio Restaurant, overlooking the Duomo.

I worked as the sole videographer for the event, handling filming, editing, color grading, and post-production to deliver the official aftermovie.
